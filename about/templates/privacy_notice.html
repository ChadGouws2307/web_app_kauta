Kauta Technologies respects your privacy and will take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information. No personal identification information, such as your name or email address, is ever required or stored in this process, and your details are never sold to any third party. The collection and usage of this data can be disabled at any time by visiting www.google.com/settings/ads and changing your preferences. More information about this can be found at https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922?hl=en

You may change your password at any time and we recommend that you do so regularly.

All personal information supplied to us or collected by us is kept strictly confidential and will not be passed on or sold to third parties.
