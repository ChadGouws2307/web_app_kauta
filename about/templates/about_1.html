Why kauta?


Kauta is derived from the Sesotho word 'khauta', meaning Gold.
Historically, Gold has been an excellent investment. Our goal is to bring you the same value that Gold has brought investors for decades: A safe haven that reduces risk and increases reward.

Our Story


Kauta was born from an entrepreneurial need to democratize investing knowledge and financial information.
We believe that everyone should have financial freedom, so we designed this platform for you to achieve that through investing in the stock market.



Our Principles


  • Democratize Investing Knowledge and Information
  • Enable Everyone's Financial Freedom
  • Educate People about Financial Literacy and Investing
  • Build a passionate investing community
