Why kauta?


Kauta is derived from the Sesotho word 'khauta', meaning Gold.
Historically, Gold has been an excellent investment. Our goal is to bring you the same value that Gold has brought investors for decades: A safe haven that reduces risk and increases reward.

Our Story


We believe that investing knowledge and financial information should be readily available to everyone, easy to understand and effectively used for financial success.
So, we designed Kauta - the simplest, most effective platform for stock market research, financial analysis and investing knowledge.


Your financial freedom is our success.







Our Mission


  • Build simple and effective stock market tools
  • Reduce the costs of successful investing
  • Support and enable you to be financially independent
  • Educate the world about Financial Literacy and Investing
